Final two simple ideas...

  • Landslides triggered by rainfall is a very frequent phenomena in the Colombian Andes, many of them in very remote areas, where data is not available.

  • Free and open tools such as ALOS-Palsar, Google Earth, Google Earth Engine, Sentinel can be used for landslide inventories and susceptibility models, with acceptable results.
