Final ideas...

  • Landslide cluster triggered by rainfall is a very frequent phenomena in the Colombian Andes

  • Landslide triggered by rainfall is a very complex and concatenated phenomena with a very high destruction capacity, which generate moderate and big disasters

  • Much more research should be carry out in Colombia to understand this kind of landsldie clusters, to stablish early warning systems and land planning policies and reduce human and econmic losses
