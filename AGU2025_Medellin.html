Take-Home Messages

  • Urbanization is not just occupying hazardous ground; it is actively modifying and amplifying susceptibility, especially in informal contexts.
  • Static zoning based only on geology is insufficient. We need dynamic policies that address *how* settlements are built on the urban fringe.
